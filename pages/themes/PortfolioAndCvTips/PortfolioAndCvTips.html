Building Your AI Portfolio

Building Your AI Portfolio

The GitHub Presence

Your GitHub profile is your real resume.
Profile README: Use a cool template to showcase your skills and latest projects.
Pinned Repositories: Choose the 3-4 projects that show diversity (one scraper, one ML, one full-stack AI app).
Green Squares: Consistency matters. Use AI to help you commit daily (don't fake it, use it!).

The Personal Website

Move beyond GitHub. A personal site allows you to control the narrative.
Use GitHub Pages or Vercel (free) to host a simple portfolio.
Showcase your "Final Project" prominently.
Include a blog section: "How I built X using Gemini and Python."

CV Best Practices for AI

CV Best Practices for AI

Recruiters spend 6 seconds on your CV. Use keywords wisely.
Keywords: Python, LLMs, RAG, Prompt Engineering, Scikit-learn, Transformers.
Measurable Impact: "Improved chatbot response accuracy by 20% using better RAG architecture" instead of "Built a chatbot."
Tooling: Mention experience with AI-assisted development (Vibe Coding, Cursor, etc.).

The Hybrid Format

Combine chronological experience with a strong project section.
If you don't have corporate AI experience, your Projects ARE your experience.
Provide links to GitHub repos for every major project.

LinkedIn & Networking

LinkedIn & Networking

The Digital Business Card

Optimize your profile for the AI boom:
Headline: "AI Engineer | Python Specialist | Building with GenAI & LLMs."
Featured Section: Add your project demo video and your portfolio site link.
Networking: Follow AI leaders (Karpathy, Altmann) and comment on their posts with value.

Interview Preparation

Interview Preparation

The "Talking Technical" Skill

Practice explaining your projects using the STAR method:
Situation: What was the problem?
Task: What did you need to do?
Action: How did you use Python/AI to solve it?
Result: What was the outcome?
Be ready to discuss: Why Gemini over OpenAI? How do you handle hallucinations? How do you manage your API costs?

Hands-On: AI-Powered Career Audit

Hands-On: AI-Powered Career Audit

Activity: Audit Your Presence

Use an AI agent to grade your current materials:
1. Paste your current CV/LinkedIn summary into the prompt.
2. Ask: "Acting as a FAANG AI recruiter, give me 3 critical improvements for this profile to target AI Engineer roles in 2026."
3. Generate a new "About" section that highlights your work in this course.
Pro-tip: Ask for your ATS Score against a specific job description.
